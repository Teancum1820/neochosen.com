About Kirtland Heritage Group

Kirtland Heritage Group is a nonprofit organization dedicated to strengthening community and interfaith relationships through events, service, and local partnerships.

Our vision is to preserve Kirtland’s rich history while building a vibrant future. We support local businesses, reinvest resources into community improvements, and create meaningful experiences that inspire residents and visitors alike.
