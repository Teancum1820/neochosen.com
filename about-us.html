ABOUT US

The Kirtland Heritage Group is a non-profit organization that supports community and interfaith projects in Kirtland, Ohio, and its neighboring communities.

Our Vision

The Kirtland Heritage Group is dedicated to preserving Kirtland’s historically rich past while building a vibrant future for our community. Through year-round events and initiatives, we celebrate the city’s unique heritage, strengthen interfaith and civic relationships, and generate funding for meaningful community improvements.

We seek to keep tourism dollars local, supporting small businesses and fueling the local economy, while providing opportunities to beautify, enrich, and uplift Kirtland in ways that honor its historic significance and inspire future generations.
